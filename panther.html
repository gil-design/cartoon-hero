Black Panther

About the Panther

A king from the hidden country of Wakanda. The title of Black Panther is passed on from each generation by ingesting a heart shaped herb gaining super human abilities

The Black Panther speaking with past kings

Black Panther's Abilities

  1. Power to draw knoweldge and strength from all previous Black Panthers
  2. Superhuman strength, speed, agility, reflexs, endurance... he's a beast
  3. Master martial artist and tactician
  4. A bad ass suit made of vibranium
